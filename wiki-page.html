The Brown Bear

The brown bear is a large bear species found across Eurasia and North America. It is one of the largest living terrestrial members of the order Carnivora.

Top 3 Characteristics

  1. Strong and powerful build
  2. Excellent sense of smell
  3. Omnivorous diet

  • Mountains
